---
term: "Comprehensive Data Inventory (CDI)"
slug: "comprehensive-inventory"
definition: "An inventory of all data assets held by a federal agency, typically stored in a JSON format. An agency comprehensive inventory is usually the source of data used by Data.gov harvesters to populate the Federal Data Catalog. Source: [M-25-05](https://whitehouse.gov/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/M-25-05-Phase-2-Implementation-of-the-Foundations-for-Evidence-Based-Policymaking-Act-of-2018-Open-Government-Data-Access-and-Management-Guidance.pdf) and [44 U.S.C. § 3502](https://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/44/3502)"
